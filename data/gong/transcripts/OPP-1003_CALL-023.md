**Opportunity:** OPP-1003

Leah Tan: Any package change still needs precise terms. If an add-on is deferred, the order form must say what is included now and what is not.

Mateo Ruan: And include the risk that a large discount could reduce confidence in the platform's strategic value.

Leah Tan: Include the risk that unapproved language creates legal exposure.

Priya Sato: That is the brief I need.

Leah Tan: Exactly. A good summary preserves constraints.
